Army Background

The SA Army offers a 2-year MSDS program with professional and functional military training.

Army Requirements

  • RSA Citizen (No dual citizenship)
  • Age 18 – 22 (Grade 12), 18 – 26 (Tertiary)
  • APS 17+, No criminal record, medically fit
  • No visible tattoos
{% elif branch == 'navy' %}

Navy Background

The SA Navy MSDS allows youth to serve on vessels or in technical/naval roles with high discipline.

Navy Requirements

  • RSA Citizen (No dual citizenship)
  • Age 18 – 22 (Grade 12), 18 – 26 (N6/National Diploma)
  • Swimming ability required
  • No criminal record, medically fit
{% elif branch == 'airforce' %}

Airforce Background

The SA Air Force offers MSDS recruits training in aviation, radar, technical and logistics roles.

Airforce Requirements

  • RSA Citizen
  • 18 – 22 (Grade 12), up to 26 with tertiary education
  • APS 17+ excluding Life Orientation
  • Height: Min 1.6m, Vision: Min 6/6
